About me

Hello, my name is Noel John Recto, and I am a 22-year-old graduated in De La Salle John Bosco College with a degree in Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. I am passionate about programming and technology, and I enjoy discovering new knowledge about these fields whenever I have free time. My favorite hobbies include playing online games, watching movies, and exploring different programming languages.
